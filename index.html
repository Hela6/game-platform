Arctic Tac Toe

Welcome to the North Pole ! Arctic-tac-toe is a game for two players, a polar bear and a seal, who take turns marking the spaces in a 3 x 3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game. Put on your Parka and be careful with the water splashes, it's cold out there !
